Rishav is a passionate developer with expertise in creating intuitive and accessible web applications. With a background in both design and development, he specializes in crafting user-friendly interfaces that are both beautiful and functional.

About Rishav

Rishav Srivastava has been working in web development for over 8 years, focusing on creating intuitive user interfaces and accessible web applications. His passion for design and color theory led to the creation of ColorViz, a tool that aims to make color selection and visualization easier for everyone.

With a background in both design and development, Rishav bridges the gap between aesthetics and functionality. He believes that good design should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or technical knowledge.

When not coding, Rishav enjoys photography, exploring color in the natural world, and contributing to open-source projects that promote web accessibility.
