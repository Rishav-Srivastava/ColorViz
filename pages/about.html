About ColorViz

A modern color selection and visualization tool to help you create stunning, accessible designs.

Our Mission

ColorViz was created to help designers and developers visualize color combinations easily. Our goal is to make color selection intuitive and accessible for everyone.

Accessibility Focus

We believe that beautiful design should be accessible to all. Our tools help ensure your color choices meet WCAG accessibility standards for contrast and readability.

For Creators

Whether you're a professional designer, developer, or hobbyist, our tools are built to help you make better color decisions for your projects quickly and confidently.

Our Story

ColorViz was born from a simple idea: making color selection and visualization accessible to everyone. We noticed that many designers and developers struggle with choosing colors that not only look good but also maintain proper contrast and readability.

Our tool provides an intuitive interface to experiment with colors and immediately see how they perform in real-world scenarios. We focus on both aesthetics and accessibility, ensuring that your design choices are beautiful and functional for all users.

Whether you're a professional designer, a developer, or just someone who wants to explore color combinations, ColorViz is designed with you in mind. We continue to improve our tools based on user feedback and emerging best practices in design.
