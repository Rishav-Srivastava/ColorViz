Text on White Background

Text Color:

The purpose of this preview is to help you visualize how your selected color performs as text on a white background. Good color contrast is essential for readability and accessibility. The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) recommend a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text to ensure it's readable by people with visual impairments. Experiment with different colors to find the perfect balance between aesthetics and usability.

Background Color Preview

Background:

This preview demonstrates how text appears when placed on your selected background color. When choosing background colors, consider both white and dark text options to ensure optimal contrast. For darker backgrounds, white or light-colored text typically works best. The visual appeal of your design is important, but never compromise on readability. A good rule of thumb is to make sure text stands out clearly against its background.
